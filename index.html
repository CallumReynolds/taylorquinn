Hi there!

My name is Taylor and I have just graduated with a Bachelor of Arts and Education from The University of Queensland. My teaching areas are English and Drama at a high school level. I have attended placement at Bundamba State Secondary College, Marsden State High School, Yeronga State High School, and Indooroopilly State High School. Each of these opportunities taught me a lot and I am excited to put my practice and theory to good use in the classroom! I am currently looking for a teaching position and am so excited for the year ahead as I begin my teaching career.

    Drama Teaching and Learning Plan (TLAP)

    This TLAP was written for and used to teach the Unit 3 - Challenge, IA2 assessment that asks students to create a dramatic concept. It includes a unit overview, detailed lesson plans for key lessons, Key resources such as PowerPoints and an exemplar and a rationale. This TLAP recieved a 7/7 when graded by my university tutor.
